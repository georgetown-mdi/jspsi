# Design notes

Tracked but exploratory. Files here are working notes and design musings - they
capture reasoning and options under consideration, not decisions or
specifications. Nothing here is binding: a note may graduate into an Architecture
Decision Record (a ratified decision with its context, alternatives, and status),
be folded into the formal docs once a direction is actually chosen, or simply be
discarded.

The maturity ladder:

- `scratch/` (gitignored) - personal, throwaway thinking, no audience.
- `docs/notes/` (here) - tracked, exploratory, citeable; no decision implied.
- `docs/adr/` - ratified decisions with their alternatives and status (not yet
  established; the first note to warrant it would start the convention).
- `docs/` proper - the formal, living specifications.

Naming: notes use lowercase-kebab filenames to distinguish them at a glance from
the SCREAMING_CASE formal specs in `docs/`.
